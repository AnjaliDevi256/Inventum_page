About Us

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology & Sciences (ANITS), Visakhapatnam is owned by Megha Engineering and Infrastructure Limited (MEIL) which is one of the top infrastructure and manufacturing company in India with headquarters at Hyderabad. The institute was established in the academic year 2001-02 by Anil Neerukonda Educational Society (ANES) which was founded by Dr. N.B.R. Prasad, an NRI Philanthropist from the USA, with industrialists and eminent educationists in memory of Late Anil Neerukonda, S/o. Dr. N.B.R. Prasad.

Establishment and Recognition:

Established in 2001, ANITS is approved by AICTE and the Government of Andhra Pradesh, and is permanently affiliated with Andhra University.

Accreditations:

It is accredited by NAAC with an 'A+' grade and by NBA for several of its engineering programs.

Autonomous Status:

ANITS gained autonomous status in 2015.

Rankings:

ANITS was ranked in the NIRF 2022 band of 251-300 for engineering institutions.

Courses Offered:

ANITS offers B.Tech programs in various disciplines, including Computer Science Engineering, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Data Science, and Electronics & Communication Engineering. It also offers postgraduate programs like M.Tech with specializations in AI & ML, Data Science, VLSI & Embedded Systems, and Machine Design

Specifics about the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department:

  • The CSE department has been accredited by NBA.
  • The department offers specializations in AI & ML and Data Science.
  • The CSE department has seen increases in student intake over the years, with expansions in the AI & ML program

Message from HOD

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is committed to preparing students for the dynamic and evolving demands of the tech industry. With a team of experienced faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories, and an industry-aligned curriculum, the department emphasizes both core computing skills and advanced AI/ML technologies such as deep learning, data science, natural language processing, and large language models (LLMs). We foster a culture of innovation, research, and practical learning through hands-on projects, internships, and technical events. Our comprehensive education framework encourages problem-solving, career preparation, lifelong learning, and ethical responsibility. The department aims to develop future-ready professionals who are not only technically proficient but also socially responsible and ethically grounded.

Message from HOD

Prof. Adinarayana Salina is a passionate teacher, a distinguished expert in the field of Information Technology, and an academic administrator with over 26 years of experience. He obtained his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Pondicherry University (a Central University). He regularly delivers expert talks at various universities and engineering institutions across the country. Currently, he is serving as a Professor in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (Data Science). Prof. Salina has published more than 70 research papers, holds four patents, and has applied for several research projects, particularly in the area of Machine Learning. He also serves as an editor and reviewer for various reputed research journals and conferences. In recognition of his academic excellence and contributions to the field, he was honoured with the Jyestha Acharya Award by the former Vice-Chancellor of Andhra University, Prof. P.V.G.D. Prasad Reddy, and APSCHE Chairman, Prof. K. Hemachandra Reddy. He has received appreciations from industries and academic institutions for his valuable contributions and is currently guiding three Ph.D. research scholars.
